About Bunmi Adelugba & Co.

Bunmi Adelugba & Co. is a professional practice of Chartered Accountants and Tax Practitioners headquartered in Abuja. Our firm is built on a commitment to delivering quality audit, accounting, tax, and advisory services to a diverse clientele ranging from private businesses to NGOs and public institutions.

Why Choose Us?

Our core strength lies in our ability to combine local understanding with global best practices. We’ve assembled a multidisciplinary team of accountants, consultants, and tax specialists who are not only technically sound, but also passionate about providing client-centric solutions that help you thrive in a competitive marketplace.
